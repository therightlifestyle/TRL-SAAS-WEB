FAQ

Questions Answered

Everything you want to know about TRL.

What is TRL? +

TRL — The Right Lifestyle — is an operating system for ambitious people. It combines goal tracking, business management, AI guidance, education, and community into one unified platform designed to turn ambition into execution.

When will TRL launch? +

TRL is actively being built right now. The first version of the SaaS platform is scheduled for launch within the next 6 months. Sign up for early access to be among the first to use it and shape its development.

Who is TRL built for? +

TRL is built for ambitious people — students, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and anyone who wants to build a better life with better systems. If you have goals and want to actually achieve them, TRL is for you.

Will there be a free version? +

Yes. TRL will offer a free tier with core features, a Pro plan at $19/month for advanced tools, and an Empire plan at $49/month for the full AI-powered experience. Early access members will receive special pricing.

How can I get involved or invest? +

TRL is open to strategic partnerships, early investors, and collaborators who believe in the mission. Reach out directly to Rashid at rashidmuhammadamir@gmail.com or via WhatsApp to start a conversation.

What makes TRL different? +

Most tools solve one problem. TRL solves the whole system. Personal growth, business management, AI assistance, education, and community — all connected in one platform. No other product combines all of these for ambitious people specifically.
